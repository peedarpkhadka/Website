Zodiac Signs

The zodiac is a circle of 12 signs, each tied to a segment of the calendar year and influenced by the sun’s position at your birth. These signs reveal core traits, strengths, and quirks that make you unique. Here’s a detailed look at each one:

  • Aries (♈) – March 21 to April 19
    Bold and fiery, Aries are natural trailblazers. They thrive on adventure, often diving headfirst into challenges with a competitive edge. Ruled by Mars, they’re passionate but can be impatient.
    Fun Fact: Aries is the first sign of the zodiac, symbolizing new beginnings.
  • Taurus (♉) – April 20 to May 20
    Steady and grounded, Taurus loves comfort and stability. They’re practical, loyal, and have a keen eye for beauty, though their stubborn streak is legendary. Venus rules this sign, enhancing their sensual side.
    Common Misconception: They’re not lazy—just deliberate and paced.
  • Gemini (♊) – May 21 to June 20
    Geminis are the social butterflies of the zodiac—witty, curious, and versatile. Ruled by Mercury, they excel in communication but may struggle with focus due to their dual nature.
    Fun Fact: The twins symbolize their adaptability and ever-shifting interests.
  • Cancer (♋) – June 21 to July 22
    Deeply emotional and nurturing, Cancers are the caregivers. Ruled by the Moon, they’re intuitive and home-loving, though their moods can ebb and flow like tides.
    Common Misconception: Their sensitivity isn’t weakness—it’s their superpower.
  • Leo (♌) – July 23 to August 22
    Leos shine bright with confidence and charisma. Ruled by the Sun, they’re born leaders who love attention, but their warmth balances any perceived arrogance.
    Fun Fact: The lion emblem reflects their regal, courageous spirit.
  • Virgo (♍) – August 23 to September 22
    Analytical and meticulous, Virgos are the perfectionists. Mercury rules them, fueling their sharp minds and helpful nature, though they can nitpick.
    Common Misconception: They’re not fussy—just striving for excellence.
  • Libra (♎) – September 23 to October 22
    Libras seek balance and beauty in everything. Ruled by Venus, they’re charming peacemakers who adore relationships, but indecision can trip them up.
    Fun Fact: The scales represent their quest for fairness.
  • Scorpio (♏) – October 23 to November 21
    Intense and magnetic, Scorpios dive deep into life. Ruled by Pluto (and traditionally Mars), they’re passionate and secretive, with unmatched loyalty.
    Common Misconception: Their mystery isn’t aloofness—it’s depth.
  • Sagittarius (♐) – November 22 to December 21
    Free-spirited and optimistic, Sagittarians chase adventure. Jupiter rules them, boosting their love for exploration, though their bluntness can surprise.
    Fun Fact: The archer aims for truth and wisdom.
  • Capricorn (♑) – December 22 to January 19
    Ambitious and disciplined, Capricorns build success step-by-step. Saturn rules them, giving them a serious edge, but they’re warm beneath the surface.
    Common Misconception: They’re not cold—just focused.
  • Aquarius (♒) – January 20 to February 18
    Visionary and quirky, Aquarians think outside the box. Ruled by Uranus, they’re innovative and independent, though they can seem aloof.
    Fun Fact: The water bearer pours out ideas, not emotions.
  • Pisces (♓) – February 19 to March 20
    Dreamy and empathetic, Pisces feel everything deeply. Neptune rules them, enhancing their creativity and intuition, though they may drift into fantasy.
    Common Misconception: They’re not lost—just soulful.
