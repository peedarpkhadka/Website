Astrological Charts

A birth chart (or natal chart) is your personal cosmic blueprint, mapping the sky at your birth. It places the sun, moon, and planets across 12 zodiac signs and 12 houses, revealing how these energies play out in your life. Think of it as a celestial personality test!

The chart is divided into 12 houses, each representing different life areas. The planets in these houses influence your personality, relationships, and life events. For example, if Venus (love) is in your 7th house (partnerships), you might find love through close relationships.

The 12 Houses Explained:

  1. 1st House: Self—your identity, appearance, and first impressions.
  2. 2nd House: Value—money, possessions, and self-worth.
  3. 3rd House: Communication—thoughts, siblings, and short trips.
  4. 4th House: Home—family, roots, and your foundation.
  5. 5th House: Creativity—romance, fun, and self-expression.
  6. 6th House: Duty—health, work, and daily routines.
  7. 7th House: Partnerships—relationships and commitments.
  8. 8th House: Transformation—intimacy, shared resources, and change.
  9. 9th House: Exploration—travel, learning, and big ideas.
  10. 10th House: Ambition—career, status, and public life.
  11. 11th House: Community—friends, groups, and dreams.
  12. 12th House: Subconscious—secrets, spirituality, and closure.

(Picture a wheel split into 12 slices, each house hosting planets and signs—like Jupiter in the 9th house hinting at a love for travel.)

To read your chart, note where planets fall. For example, Saturn in the 7th house might mean serious relationships, while Mercury in the 3rd boosts communication skills. Free online tools can generate your chart—just input your birth date, time, and place.
